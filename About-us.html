About Us

At Velox, we believe you deserve to spend more on yourself and less on rides without
compromising your style and comfort while you go about your daily activities.

As our brand name implies "Velox" means quick or rapid. We offer you fast,
comfortable, safe, economical and reliable rides while providing friendly environment.
Pick-up time will not be more than 5mins apart. "why Velox?" we understand that our
customers are meant to be comfortable which is the reason we operate at our highest
level of performance and efficiency and always help drivers earn.

Whether you are sitted at the back seat or behind the wheel, your safety is essential.
We are committed to doing our part which is to provide friendly environment with
technology being at the heart of our approach, we partner with safety advocates
and develop new technologies and systems to improve safety and make it easier
for everyone to get around.
